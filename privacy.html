Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to all browser extensions published by this developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our extensions do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

Local Data Storage

All data required for the extension to function, such as user settings, preferences, or user-generated lists (e.g., a list of watched videos), is saved locally on your computer using the browser's built-in storage. Nothing is ever sent to any remote servers. All data remains on your device.

Permissions Justification

This extension requests permission to access https://www.youtube.com/* pages. This permission is required solely to enable the extension's core features, which involves reading the page structure and modifying the YouTube user interface (e.g., hiding specific videos or adding interface elements). No data from the page is collected or shared externally.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, handle, or transmit:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.)
  • Authentication, financial, or health information
  • Your location or Browse history
  • Personal communications or user activity (clicks, keystrokes, etc.)
  • Any content from the websites you visit

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the extension's store listing page.
